Swimming pool:

A swimming pool with the counter-flow.Long ago people have noticed that cooling is necessary for a hot body. Just like a steel to become stronger is removed from a hot forge into an icy water, so the human body moves from the hot air of the steam room into a cool pool.Bathing in the snow or water is an essential part of visiting any steam room. That is why the swimming pool in the sauna is its integral and very important part. It is not as spectacular as a steam room, and keeps somewhat in the shade, but it cares seriously about our health. After all, thanks to it, we temper, and, therefore, become stronger and more enduring, forgetting about the ailments.
